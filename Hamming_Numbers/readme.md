# Hamming Number

Hamming numbers are numbers of the form  

       H  =  2i × 3j × 5k 

where

        i, j, k  ≥  0 

Hamming numbers   are also known as   ugly numbers   and also   5-smooth numbers   (numbers whose prime divisors are less or equal to 5).
